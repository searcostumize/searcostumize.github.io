What is Android?

Android is an operating system for mobile devices developed by Google. It is based on the Linux kernel and is designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets.

Androids in Pop Culture

Androids have been a popular theme in pop culture, including movies, TV shows, and video games. One notable example is the video game "Detroit: Become Human." In this game, players explore a future where androids with advanced artificial intelligence struggle for their rights and autonomy.

"Detroit: Become Human" is known for its compelling storytelling and multiple branching narratives that allow players to make choices that impact the outcome of the game.
